Welcome to Hunting Energy Services Singapore

Hunting Energy Services Pte. Ltd. is a leading provider of specialized equipment and components for the oil and gas industry. With decades of experience, we serve clients across Asia and globally, delivering quality, reliability, and technical excellence.

About Us

Incorporated in 1980 and headquartered in Singapore, we are part of the global Hunting Energy Services group. We specialize in manufacturing, supplying, and maintaining high-quality oilfield and gasfield machinery and accessories. Our commitment is to help the energy industry operate safely and efficiently.

Our Services

Equipment Manufacturing

Manufacturing of high-quality threaded pipes, tool joints, and other oilfield equipment to meet international standards.

Supply & Distribution

Supplying metal products and specialized components for oil and gas operations across Asia and globally.

Maintenance & Support

Repair and maintenance services to ensure your equipment remains reliable and efficient in demanding conditions.
